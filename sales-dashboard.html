What We Track

We Track:

  • Traffic source and volume
  • Click-through rate per post
  • Email opt-in rate per post
  • Revenue per post per month
  • System age (months live)

We Ignore:

  • Follower counts
  • Like-to-comment ratios
  • Viral spikes
  • Platform-specific vanity metrics

Why: Only tracked metrics improve. We track what drives revenue.

Month One Feels Slow. Month Twelve Feels Automatic.
