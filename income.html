Income Models That Compound

Set once. Earn repeatedly. Optimize slowly.

Affiliate Marketing

The foundation. Every review, comparison, and tutorial includes affiliate links at natural decision points. No hard sells. No pop-ups. Just relevant recommendations when readers are ready to decide.

How it compounds:

  • Month 1: 10 posts, 3 conversions each
  • Month 6: 60 posts, 5 conversions each
  • Month 12: 120 posts, 8 conversions each

The math: More content × Higher trust × More traffic = Exponential revenue.

Email Sequences

Once your niche trusts your recommendations, they'll buy your systems. Templates, checklists, SOPs, tool lists. Created once. Sold forever. Integrated directly into your comparison tables.

How it compounds:

  • First product: $29, 5 sales/month
  • Sixth month: 3 products, 40 sales/month
  • Twelfth month: 5 products, 120 sales/month

The math: Product variety × Email list size × Trust level = Asset value.

Evergreen Pages

Comparison tables, resource lists, and ultimate guides rank in search for years. Updated quarterly. Earn daily. Each page is an asset that appreciates with age and backlinks.

How it compounds:

  • Month 1: 5 pages, 10 visits/day each
  • Month 6: 20 pages, 50 visits/day each
  • Month 12: 40 pages, 100 visits/day each

The math: Page count × Search traffic × Conversion rate = Long-tail revenue.

The Whole System Earns, Not Just Parts

Affiliate revenue funds content creation. Email list builds trust. Digital products monetize trust. Evergreen pages compound search traffic. Each model feeds the others. The system is the asset.
